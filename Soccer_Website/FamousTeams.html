Arsenal FC (England)

Founded in 1886, Arsenal is one of the most iconic English football clubs. Known for their attacking style and youth development, Arsenal went unbeaten in the Premier League 2003–04 season. The club plays at the Emirates Stadium in London.

Real Madrid (Spain)

With 14 UEFA Champions League titles, Real Madrid is the most successful European club. They have had legends like Cristiano Ronaldo, Zidane, and Raúl. Their rivalry with Barcelona is known as "El Clásico".

FC Barcelona (Spain)

Barcelona is known for its tiki-taka passing style and legendary players like Lionel Messi, Xavi, and Iniesta. They play at Camp Nou, one of the largest stadiums in the world.

Bayern Munich (Germany)

The dominant force in German football, Bayern Munich has won the Bundesliga over 30 times and multiple Champions League trophies. They are known for discipline, strength, and powerful tactics.

Boca Juniors (Argentina)

One of South America's most successful teams, Boca Juniors has a fierce rivalry with River Plate. Their stadium, La Bombonera, is legendary for its passionate atmosphere.
