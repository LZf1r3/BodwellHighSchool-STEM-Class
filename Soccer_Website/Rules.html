Basic Rules

  • Each team has 11 players: 10 field players and 1 goalkeeper.
  • The match lasts 90 minutes, split into two halves of 45 minutes each.
  • A goal is scored when the whole ball crosses the goal line between the posts.
  • Players cannot use their hands, except goalkeepers inside their own penalty area.
  • The offside rule prevents attackers from gaining unfair advantages.

Fouls and Free Kicks

Common fouls include tripping, pushing, or handling the ball deliberately. When a foul occurs, the referee may award a direct or indirect free kick, or a penalty kick if it happens in the penalty box.

Cards

  • Yellow card: A warning for unsporting behavior.
  • Red card: The player is sent off and cannot be replaced.

Throw-ins, Goal Kicks, and Corner Kicks

These restart the game when the ball goes out of play. The team that didn't touch the ball last gets possession.
