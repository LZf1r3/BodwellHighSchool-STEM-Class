Science Behind Crazy Dreams

Age Sleep Time Room Temperature Weird Dream %
0-5 yrs 11-14 hrs 20–22°C 15% (Simple dreams)
6-13 yrs 9-11 hrs 19–21°C 25% (Vivid imaginations)
14–17 yrs 8–10 hrs 18–20°C 60% (Hormones + stress)
18–29 yrs 7–9 hrs 17–19°C 75% (Busy minds + dreams)
30–49 yrs 6.5–8 hrs 16–18°C 55% (Dreams fade slightly)
50–64 yrs 6–7.5 hrs 18–20°C 40% (Lighter sleep)
65+ yrs 6–7 hrs 20–22°C 30% (Rare but strange)
